Verification Requirements

For Healthcare Providers

  • Valid medical license
  • Medical degree from accredited institution
  • Board certifications (if applicable)
  • Professional liability insurance
  • At least 2 professional references
  • Clean background check

For Pharmacies

  • Valid pharmacy license
  • DEA registration number
  • Pharmacist-in-charge credentials
  • Business registration documents
  • Insurance and bonding certificates
  • Compliance with state regulations

Security & Privacy

  • HIPAA compliance training
  • Data security certification
  • Background screening
  • Professional conduct agreement
  • Patient privacy commitment
  • Emergency response protocols

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the verification process take?

The verification process typically takes 2-3 business days. However, it may take longer if additional documentation or clarification is needed.

What happens if my verification is rejected?

If your verification is rejected, we'll provide detailed feedback on what needs to be corrected. You can resubmit your application with the required changes.

Can I start accepting patients before verification is complete?

No, for patient safety and legal compliance, you must complete the verification process before accepting any appointments through our platform.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we use bank-level encryption and are fully HIPAA compliant. Your documents are stored securely and only accessed by authorized verification personnel.

Do I need to renew my verification?

Yes, verification needs to be renewed annually or when your licenses/certifications are updated. We'll send you reminders before expiration.
